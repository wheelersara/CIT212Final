Other Considerations

  • Make sure that you have the appropriate vaccinations that certain countries might reccomend.
  • Consider currency conversions, and which currancy you will be using in the county you are visiting. Make your bank aware that you are traveling and carry some cash.
  • Be aware of your surroundings, and stay with a tour guide in unfamiliar places. Be aware of dangerous locations and common crimes.
  • Make an agenda and let your family at home know of your plans.
  • Get travel insurance, you never know when you may face an emergency abroad!
  • Make yourself aware of the culture and traditions in the country you are visiting. Be mindful and respectful to the locals.
