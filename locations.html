#1 Travel Locations

Home Locations Packing Essentials

Fairbanks, Alaska

Fairbanks Alaska is known for it's spectacular views of the northern lights. Located in central Alaska, it has an arctic landscape. Some activities to do here is dog sledding, skiing, and hiking.

Grand Canyon, Arizona

The Grand Canyon, located in Arizona is one of the 7 Natural Wonders of the World, and one of the most impressive national parks. You can see the beautiful scenery of the canyon and the Colorado River.

Mt.Fuji Japan

Mt. Fuji is technically not a mountain, but an active volcano located in Japan. Located west of Tokyo, it is an must see feature on a trip to Japan. The best time to visit is late March to early April during cherry blossom season.

Santorini, Greece

Santorini, Greece is an island of Greece, is well known for it's white and blue Cycladic architecture. Located on an Island, it has beautiful coastal views.

Venice, Italy

Venice Italy captures the image that comes to mind when you think of Italy. It is home to stunning architecture, canals and waterways, delicious food, and iconic history.

Ring of Kerry, Ireland

The Ring of Kerry in Ireland is a scenic road that captures traditional Irish culture, as well as the beautiful scenery that Ireland has to offer. It offers a diverse range of scenery, from rolling hills, coasts, mountains and plains, it is truly a sight to see!

Efurt, Germany

Efurt, Germany is home to one of the best Christkindlmarks in Germany. A must visit in the wintertime, the stunning traditional German architecture of the village captures the Christmas spirit.

Swiss Alps, Switzerland

The Swiss Alps is a beautiful, notorious mountain range in Switzerland. Some must-do activities include riding the train for a scenic tour of Switzerland, hiking, or visiting ski lodges.

Scottish Highlands, Scotland

The Scottish Highlands are home to gorgeous green hilly landscapes, lochs, castles, and a picturesqe scenery. The best time to visit is May through September. They run from Helensburgh to Stonehaven, Scotland.

Tasmania, Australia

Tasmania is an island state off the south coast of Australia. It is home to beautiful mountains, coastlines, and unique wildlife. It is possible to see the Aurora Australis, which is the southern hemisphere's version of the northern lights.
